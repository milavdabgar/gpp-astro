Successful Entrepreneurs of Institute

Alumni Success Stories

Our alumni have gone on to establish successful ventures across various industries, from technology startups to manufacturing companies. Their achievements serve as inspiration for current students.

  • • Technology startups in AI/ML and IoT
  • • Manufacturing and industrial solutions
  • • Healthcare and biotechnology ventures
  • • Sustainable energy and green technology
  • • E-commerce and digital platforms

Mentorship Program

Our successful alumni actively participate in mentoring current students, providing guidance on business development, funding, and scaling operations.

Contact: For mentorship opportunities and alumni connections, please reach out to the Entrepreneurship Cell office.
